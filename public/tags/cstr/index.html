CSTR

Deep Neural Network Nonlinear Model Predictive Control for CSTR

The Continuous Stirred Tank Reactor (CSTR) is a fundamental model in chemical en- gineering, representing a reactor where the contents are well-mixed, ensuring uniform composition and temperature throughout. This report delves into the mathematical modeling of a CSTR, its simulation, and control using a Nonlinear Model Predictive Control (NMPC) strategy integrated with a neural network for predictive behavior.
